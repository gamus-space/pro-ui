About

This is a sequel to a mainstream real-time strategy game. The world war between Allies and Soviets continues.

Music

The music is composed by:

  • Frank Klepacki

The music is the same for both sides.

The game features a jukebox, allowing to select track during gameplay.

The music is stored in ADPCM-compressed WAV files. It has quality of 22kHz 16bit stereo. Exceptional 2 tracks are stored in AUD proprietary format with quality of 22kHz 16bit mono.

Graphics

The game allows to pick a resolution of 640x480 (featured), 800x600 or 1024x768 pixels with 64k colors.
