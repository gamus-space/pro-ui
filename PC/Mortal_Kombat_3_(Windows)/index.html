About

The third tournament in the series. Shao Kahn tries to conquer Earthrealm by merging it with the Outworld and so he must be stopped by the 8 Earth warriors (out of 14 competitors).

Music

Game has CD Audio music was composed by:

  • Dan Forden

The music used by the Windows version is a subset of music from DOS version.

Graphics

The game uses resolution of 320x240 pixels with 64k colors.

Version specifics

This is a port of the PlayStation version to the PC.

Comparing to the DOS release it's graphically superior but musically inferior (e.g. not all the music is played).
