Platform PC
Year 1998
Developer Electronic Arts Seattle
Publisher Electronic Arts
System Windows 95
Date added 2025-02-13

About

This is the third part of a mainstream racing game. It features 15 licensed sports cars.

Music

The music is composed by:

  • Alistair Hirst
  • Matt Ragan
  • Crispin Hands
  • Saki Kaskas
  • Rom Di Prisco

Music is stored in proprietary MAP/MUS and ASF files with 22kHz 16bit stereo quality.

The game changes music based on race conditions.

Graphics

Featured version uses 3DFX card providing 640x480 resolution with 64k colors.

Screenshots present the tracks on default conditions (like Beginner Tournament):

  • forward
  • non-mirrored
  • day time
  • standard weather

The Hot Pursuit mode is not included.

Others

In total there are 9 tracks * 16 conditions = 144 different routes. This game is huge!

Also in 1998 an official downloadable content pack featuring 4 new cars was released (it is not included).
