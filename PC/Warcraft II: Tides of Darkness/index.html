About

The Second War is the Orcs attempt to conquer Lordaeron which hosts the survivors of Azeroth after the First War. Now both sides form greater alliances.

Music

Each side has their specific music and menu uses Orc theme.

The game can play CD Audio tracks or MIDI music.

The OPL3 version is available at gamus.space.
