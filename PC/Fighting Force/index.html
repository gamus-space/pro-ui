About

This is an early 3D beat 'em up game featuring 4 characters:

  • Alana
  • Hawk
  • Mace
  • Smasher

whose quest is to find and defeat evil Doctor Zeng.

Music

Game has CD Audio tracks composed by:

  • Martin Iveson

Graphics

Featured version uses 3DFX card providing 640x480 resolution with 64k colors.

Version specifics

The PC version does not have credits roll.
