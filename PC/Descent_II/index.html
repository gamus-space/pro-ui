About

The sequel to Descent is also a first-person shooter game with six degrees of freedom.

Player is the pilot in control of the spaceship equipped with various weapons.

The aim of the mission is to destroy reactors and escape from explosions in 24 regular levels and 6 secret levels divided in 6 worlds.

Music

Game has CD Audio tracks composed by:

  • Brian Luzietti
  • Larry Peacock, Brad Cross, Leslie Spitzer
  • Ogre of Skinny Puppy, Mark Walk
  • Johann Langlie
  • Ron Valdez

There is composer information for individual tracks available on the Internet.

The game also contains MIDI music and the OPL3 version can be listened to at gamus.space.

Graphics

The featured 3DFX version uses 640x480 resolution with 64k colors.
