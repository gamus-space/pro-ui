About

The is an expansion pack for Diablo II featuring:

  • new character classes
  • new locations
  • new monsters
  • new items
  • usability facilitations

The new quest is to defeat Baal, who is the Lord of Destruction.

Music

The game has the soundtrack in packaged 22kHz 16bit stereo WAV files composed by:

  • Matt Uelmen

Graphics

The game uses resolution of 640x480 pixels with 256 colors.

The expansion also allows to use 800x600 resolution.

Version specifics

The PC version was the base release.
