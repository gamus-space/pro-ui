About

This is the third entry in the Prince of Persia series, now in full 3D and echoing themes from the first game.

The Prince sets out to rescue his wife after she is kidnapped by the warlord Rugnor.

Music

The music was composed by:

  • Jonelle Adkisson
  • Greg Rahn

Music is stored in uncompressed raw files with quality of 22kHz 16-bit stereo.

Presented tracks are linear music dumps, whereas the game plays a subsong at a time.

Graphics

The game supports Direct3D with resolutions of 640x480, 800x600 and 1024x768. Screenshots use 3DFX renderer with 640x480 resolution and 64k colors.

Version specifics

Version 1.0 is buggy and so should be avoided. Use 1.1 or 1.2 instead.

Others

Featured version: 1.0

This game is also known as Prince of Persia: Arabian Nights.
