About

This is an expansion pack for Warcraft II: Tides of Darkness. After the Dark Portal destroyed by Humans, Orc launch a new invasion on Azeroth.

Music

The music is composed by:

  • Glenn Stafford
  • Matt Samia (additional music)

Each side has their specific music and menu uses Orc theme.

The game can play CD Audio tracks or MIDI music (without the new music).

The OPL3 version is available at gamus.space.

Graphics

The game uses resolution of 640x480 pixels with 256 colors.
