Date added 2024-08-12

About

The second expansion pack to the 3D first-perspective shooter game. The hero crawls even more dungeons to face the Dragon guarding the Temporal Energy Converter.

Expansion features new weapons, enemy types and power-ups located in 15 levels divided in 2 episodes.

Music

Game has CD Audio tracks composed by (according to game manual):

  • Jeehun Hwang

Graphics

Featured version uses 3DFX card providing 640x480 resolution with 64k colors.

Version specifics

The game was originally released for DOS with software rendering support. A year later an mini-OpenGL port was released for Windows 95, called glQuake.
