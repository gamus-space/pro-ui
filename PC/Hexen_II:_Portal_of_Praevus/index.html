About

This is an expansion pack for Hexen II. The events take place after Eidolon's defeat.

Game contains 15 new levels grouped in 2 units, new character class (Demoness) with unique weapons and some new enemies.

Music

Game has CD Audio tracks composed by:

  • Kevin Schilder (officially credited)
  • Chia Chin Lee (actual composer, according to some sources on the Internet)

The game can play CD Audio tracks or MIDI music.

The OPL3 version is available online at gamus.space.

Graphics

Featured version uses 3DFX card providing 640x480 resolution with 64k colors.
