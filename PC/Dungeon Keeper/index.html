About

This is an innovative game that combines RTS gameplay with strong dungeon-management mechanics.

The objective is to build dungeons in order to protect from hero invasion or defeat the hostile competing keeper.

Music

The game has CD audio tracks composed by:

  • Russell Shaw

Graphics

Originally game supports 320x200 or 640x480 resolutions with 256 colors. The featured Direct3D version with 3DFX renderer has resolution of 640x480 and 64k colors.

Others

Featured version: Dungeon Keeper Gold, Direct3D patch.

An expansion pack The Deeper Dungeons is available but is does not bring any new music.
