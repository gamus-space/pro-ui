About

This is the third game in Monkey Island series, now in cartoon-lie style.

After recovering from LeChuck's curse, Guybrush Threepwood drifts in his bumper car into the middle of LeChuck's siege on the hero's beloved Elaine.

Music

The music is composed by:

  • Michael Land

Music is stored in proprietary IMX files with 22kHz 16bit stereo quality which are supposed to use ADPCM compression.

The game uses hardly noticeable transitions between tracks.

Game's music engine is called iMUSE.

Track Monkey 1 is a copy of mi1_woods2.laa from The Secret of Monkey Island.

Graphics

The game uses 640x480 resolution with 256 colors.
