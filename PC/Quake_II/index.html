About

A continuation of a mainstream 3D first-perspective shooter game. This time the space marine hero fights the Stroggs.

Game features 39 levels divided in 10 units.

Music

Game has CD Audio tracks composed by:

  • Sonic Mayhem
  • Jer Sypult
  • Bill Brown (according to various sources in the web)

For some reason, after several minutes of gameplay the music switches to track 11.

Graphics

Featured version uses 3DFX card providing 640x480 resolution with 64k colors.

Version specifics

The PC version is the reference version.
