About

MDK is a third-person perspective 3D shooter/platform game. Kurt Hectic is saving the Earth from invasion of city-sized vehicles called Minecrawlers.

Music

The game has a soundtrack composed by:

  • Todd Dennis (according to wikipedia)
  • Tommy Tallarico Studios (according to game manual)

The music is stored in disappointing 8-22kHz 8/16bit mono uncompressed WAV files.

Graphics

All the versions use 640x480 resolution with 256 colors. The 3D accelerated patches (3DFX, Direct3D) upgrade to 64k colors.
