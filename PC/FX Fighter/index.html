About

An early 3D fighting game, released for the PC only. Eight competitors fight to be the best in the universe:

  • Magnon from Inferno
  • Sheba from Rhomb
  • Venam from Peres
  • Jake from Sentral
  • Kiko from Lusk
  • Siren from Ursae
  • Ashraf from Karlak
  • Cyben from Axone

Music

Game has CD Audio tracks composed by:

  • Justin Scharvona

Graphics

The game runs on BRender engine displaying at 320x200 pixels with 256 colors.

Version specifics

This game was released only to PC.
