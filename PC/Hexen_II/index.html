About

This is a sequel to Hexen: Beyond Heretic.

Game consists of 37 levels divided in 5 units. The player can choose a character class from:

  • Paladin
  • Crusader
  • Necromancer
  • Assassin

In order to face Eidolon, the hero must first defeat The Four Horsemen of the Apocalypse.

Music

Game has CD Audio tracks composed by:

  • Kevin Schilder

The game can play CD Audio tracks or MIDI music.

The OPL3 version is available online at gamus.space.

The Winnowing Hall track is a remade version of Winnowing Hall from Hexen: Beyond Heretic and hence it's official name. In this game it's used in Roman levels.

Temple of Horus in turn is another version of The Docks from Heretic.

Graphics

Featured version uses 3DFX card providing 640x480 resolution with 64k colors.
