About

This is a 2D fighting game. 10 fighters compete in a street fighting tournament, which takes place between the Final Fight and Street Fighter 2 games.

Music

The music is composed by:

  • Isao Abe
  • Syun Nishigaki
  • Setsuo Yamamoto
  • Yuko Takehara
  • Naoaki Iwami
  • Naoshi Mizuta

Music is stored in uncompressed 22kHz 16bit stereo WAV files.

Graphics

The game uses a custom resolution of 360x240 pixels with 256 colors.

Version specifics

The PC version is based on the PlayStation version.
