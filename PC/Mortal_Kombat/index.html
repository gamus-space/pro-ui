About

A mainstream 2D fighting game. 7 warriors participate in a deadly tournament held by mysterious Shang-Tsung on his island.

Music

Game music was composed by:

  • Dan Forden

Music is copied from the Arcade version in very low 11kHz 8bit mono quality (uncompressed).

Graphics

The game uses resolution of 320x200 pixels with 256 colors.

Version specifics

Overall this version is very close to the original Arcade release.

The PC CD release uses original arcade soundtrack, in favor of MIDI-like arranged music from the former PC floppy release.
