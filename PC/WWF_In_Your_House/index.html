About

This is a successor of WWF WrestleMania.

Each wrestler now has a unique themed stage outside the traditional ring. The roster features 10 competitors, 3 of whom also appeared in the previous title.

Music

Game has CD Audio tracks composed by:

  • Mark Ganus
  • Dean Morrell
  • Roy Wilkins

Graphics

The game uses a resolution of 320x200 pixels and the menus 320x240 with 256 colors.
