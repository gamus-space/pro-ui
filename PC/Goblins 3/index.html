About

It's a sequel to Gobliins 2: The Prince Buffoon

The goblin reporter Blount starts a journey to country of Foliander, during which he meets new friends to assist him in his quest (including his alter ego).

Music

The CD-ROM release has a single CD Audio track composed by:

  • Charles Callet

The original floppy release has a melodic OPL3 soundtrack available to listen at gamus.space.

Graphics

The game uses 320x200 resolution with 256 colors.

Version specifics

The floppy version was also released in 1993.

As mentioned before, CD-ROM version replaced the original music. Because of added digitized voices, all subtitles were removed from the game. In addition static story screens were replaced with animations.

Others

Featured version: 1.02

This game was later released by Sierra as Goblins Quest 3.
