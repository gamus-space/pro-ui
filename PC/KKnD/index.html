About

A post-apocalyptic real-time strategy game about the conflict between:

  • Survivors - humans
  • Evolved - mutans

Both sides have a 15-mission campaign.

Music

The music is composed by:

  • Marshall Parker
  • Gavin Parker
  • Cameron Brown

Music is stored in proprietary SON format with quality of 22kHz 16bit stereo.

Each side has their specific music.

Graphics

The game uses resolution of 640x480 pixels with 256 colors.

Others

Featured version: 1.2

The full name of the game is Krush, Kill 'n' Destroy.
