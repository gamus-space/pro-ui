About

This is a shooting/platform game. The hero X with his friend Zero investigate and hunt down the Maverick rebellion, leading them to Dr. Doppler.

Music

The music is arranged by (according to wikipedia):

  • Toshihiko Horiyama
  • Shusaku Uchiyama
  • Yoshino Aoki
  • Makoto Tomozawa

Music is stored in uncompressed WAV files with quality of 22kHz 8bit stereo.

Graphics

The game uses a resolution of 320x240 pixels with 256 colors.

Version specifics

The PC version is superior to the original SNES version.
