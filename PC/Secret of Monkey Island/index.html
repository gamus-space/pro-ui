About

The game tells the story of a young man, Guybrush Threepwood, on his quest to become a pirate.

Music

The CD-ROM release has CD Audio music originally composed by:

  • Michael Land
  • Barney Jones
  • Andy Newell
  • Patrick Mundy

The original floppy release has MIDI music and the OPL3 version is available at gamus.space.

Graphics

The game uses 320x200 resolution with 256 colors.

Version specifics

The original floppy version was released in 1990.

CD-ROM release replaced MIDI music with CD Audio tracks.
