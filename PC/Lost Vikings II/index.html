About

This is a 2D puzzle/platform game. The 3 Vikings got caught again and meet 2 new friend on their escape.

Music

Music is stored in 22kHz 8bit mono uncompressed WAV files.

Graphics

The game uses a resolution of 320x240 pixels with 256 colors.

Version specifics

The PC version is superior to the original SNES version.
