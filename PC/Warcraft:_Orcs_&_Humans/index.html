About

This is a fantasy real-time strategy game. The objective is to attack Azeroth as Orcs or defend the invasion as Humans.

Music

The music is composed by:

  • Glenn Stafford
  • Gregory Alper
  • Rick Jackson
  • Chris Palmer

Each side has their specific music and menu is neutral.

The game plays MIDI music only, despite the CD having audio tracks (which are probably only a bonus content).

The OPL3 version is available at gamus.space.

Graphics

The game uses resolution of 320x200 pixels with 256 colors.

Version specifics

A noticeable difference from the floppy version are cinematic cutscenes instead of static images.
