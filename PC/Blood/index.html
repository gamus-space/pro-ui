About

This is a 3D first-perspective shooter game.

The (anti-)hero Caleb seeks revenge on his dark master who disposed him and chases the demon who kidnapped his love.

Music

Game has CD Audio tracks with ambient-style music composed by:

  • Guy Whitmore
  • Daniel Bernstein
  • Mike Cody

There is composer information for individual tracks available on the Internet.

The game supports MIDI music as well and the OPL3 version is available at gamus.space.

Graphics

The game runs in software mode using 320x200, 320x400, 640x480 (featured) or 800x600 resolution with 256 colors.

Version specifics

This version is uncensored.

Others

Featured version: 1.2

There exists a beta 3DFX patch, which is unfortunately not playable due to numerous issues.

Two expansion packs were released: Cryptic Passage and Plasma Pak but they used original CD Audio music.
