About

American tourist George Stobbart survives a bombing at a Parisian café and soon teams up with journalist Nicole Collard. Together, they follow a trail of clues that draws them into a centuries‑old conspiracy tied to the Knights Templar.

Music

Game music is composed by:

  • Barrington Pheloung

There are 253 rather short tracks of total 2 hours 45 minutes duration. Music switches contextually, mostly after entering a new location but sometimes also after an event or a specific dialogue part occur.

Music is stored in uncompressed WAV files with quality of 11kHz 16bit mono, with some individual tracks sampled at 22kHz.
