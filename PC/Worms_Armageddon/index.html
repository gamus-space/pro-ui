About

This is a continuation of a mainstream turn-based artillery game.

Third part introduces new terrain types, weapons and game modes and high res support.

Music

Game has audio tracks composed by:

  • Bjørn Lynne

Music is stored in uncompressed WAV files with quality of 16bit stereo and sampling rate varying from 16kHz to 28kHz.

Graphics

The game allows to pick resolution of 640x480 (featured), 800x600 or 1024x768 pixels with 256 colors.

Featured single player modes are Basic Training and Missions.
