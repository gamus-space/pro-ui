About

This is a real-time strategy game. It tells a story of world war between GDI (the good guys) and NOD (the bad guys) in alternate history.

Music

The music is composed by:

  • Frank Klepacki

Music is the same for both sides.

The game features a jukebox, allowing to select track during gameplay.

The music is stored in AUD proprietary format. It has quality of 22kHz 16bit mono and uses ADPCM compression.

Graphics

The game uses resolution of 320x200 pixels with 256 colors.

Version specifics

The PC version is the reference version.
