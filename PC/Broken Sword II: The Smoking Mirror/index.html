About

This is a sequel to Broken Sword: The Shadow of the Templars.

George Stobbard is knocked out and his girlfriend, Nicole Collard, is kidnapped when they arrive for a meeting with Professor Oubier. This time, they find themselves drawn into an ancient Mayan mystery.

Music

Game music is composed by:

  • Barrington Pheloung

There are 466 rather short tracks of total 3 hours 28 minutes duration. Music switches contextually, mostly after entering a new location but sometimes also after an event or a specific dialogue part occur.

Music is stored in compressed RAW files with quality of 22kHz 16bit mono.

Graphics

The game uses resolution of 640x480 pixels with 256 colors.
