About

This is the final edition of the Amiga fighting game series, combining contents of Body Blows (AGA) and Body Blows Galactic. The game features 20 competitors fighting in 10 stages.

Music

Game has CD Audio tracks composed by:

  • Allister Brimble
  • Waveform (Steve Blenkinsopp, according to some sources on the Internet)

Unfortunately there are only 6 in-game tracks (including title track).

Graphics

The game uses resolution of 320x200 pixels with 256 colors.

Version specifics

The PC CD version uses the music of Amiga CD version.

There was also PC floppy version that has Body Blows Galactic (mostly, but also Body Blows) soundtrack in mysterious SNG format.
