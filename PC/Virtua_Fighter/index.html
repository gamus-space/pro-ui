About

A 3D fighting game. Eight fighters compete in World Fighting Tournament. They are:

  • Jacky Bryant
  • Jeffry McWild
  • Sarah Bryant
  • Kagemaru
  • Pai Chan
  • Wolf Hawkfield
  • Lau Chan
  • Akira Yuki

Music

The music uses CD Audio tracks arranged by:

  • Takayuki Nakamura
  • Takenobu Mituyoshi
  • Kazuhiko Kouchi

Graphics

Only software rendering is supported in either 312x240 or 640x480 (featured) resolution with 256 colors.

Version specifics

The PC version was ported from Sega Saturn.

Others

The PC version is actually the Virtua Fighter Remix version, which is graphically superior to the original release called Virtua Fighter.

Featured version: 1.05 (Europe)
