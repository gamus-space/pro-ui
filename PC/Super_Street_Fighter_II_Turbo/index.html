Platform PC
Year 1995
Developer Eurocom
Publisher Gametek
System DOS
Date added 2024-11-10

About

A mainstream 2D fighting game. The last major variant of Street Fighter 2 games. Features 17 characters and introduces super combo moves.

Music

The CD release has CD Audio tracks arranged by:

  • Neil Baldwin
  • Steve Duckworth

The floppy release uses MIDI music and AdLib version is available at gamus.space

Graphics

The game uses a resolution of 320x200 pixels with 256 colors.
