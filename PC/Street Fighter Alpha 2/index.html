About

The game is a remake of Street Fighter Alpha, according to wikipedia. Now 18 fighters take part in the pre-Street Fighter 2 tournament.

Music

The music is composed by:

  • Syun Nishigaki
  • Setsuo Yamamoto
  • Tatsuro Suzuki

Music is stored in uncompressed 22kHz 8bit stereo WAV files.

Graphics

The game uses a custom resolution of 368x224 pixels with 256 colors.

Version specifics

The PC version is based on the PlayStation version.
