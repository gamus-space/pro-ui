Platform PC
Year 2000
Developer Core Design
Publisher Eidos Interactive
System Windows 95
Date added 2024-02-17

About

After Lara Croft learns of existence of the fifth piece of meteorite, she starts her side quest to find it.

Music

Game has CD Audio-like tracks composed by:

  • Nathan McCree

The music is stored in ADPCM-compressed WAV files of CD quality.

The expansion soundtrack is a subset of the original soundtrack.

Graphics

The game supports Direct3D, allowing to run with variety of resolutions. Screenshots use 640x480 with 64k colors.

Version specifics

The expansion pack was released exclusively to PC and Mac.

Others

Featured version: European release

This game can be also called Tomb Raider: The Lost Artifact
