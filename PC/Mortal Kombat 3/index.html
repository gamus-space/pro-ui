About

The third tournament in the series. Shao Kahn tries to conquer Earthrealm by merging it with the Outworld and so he must be stopped by the 8 Earth warriors (out of 14 competitors).

Music

Graphics

The game uses resolution of 320x200 pixels with 256 colors.

Version specifics

Overall this version would be close to the Arcade original if it hadn't used low vertical resolution, resulting in poor-looking energy bars.

The DOS version looks very polished and has some unique features available as cheats (e.g. allows to play as bosses).
