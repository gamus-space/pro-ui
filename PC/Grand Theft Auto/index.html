About

This is an innovative 3D open‑world game presented with top‑down camera views and sprite‑based objects.

The anti‑hero is an unnamed car‑thief and gun‑for‑hire climbing the criminal ladder across Liberty City, San Andreas and Vice City.

Music

The game has CD Audio soundtrack composed by:

  • Colin Anderson
  • Craig Conner
  • Grant Middleton

Music switches when changing cars and tracks are arranged to mimic radio stations.

Graphics

Game supports variety of resolutions in 256 or 64k colors. The featured 3DFX version displays 640x480 with 64k colors.

Version specifics

The PC version is the reference version.
