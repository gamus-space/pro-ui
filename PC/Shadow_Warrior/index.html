About

This is a 3D first-perspective shooter game.

In futuristic Japan Lo Wang fights his way through the Zilla Enterprises to deal with Master Zilla.

Music

Game has CD Audio tracks composed by:

  • Lee Jackson

The shareware version has MIDI music instead and the OPL3 version is available at gamus.space.

Graphics

Featured version uses 3DFX card providing 640x480 resolution with 64k colors.

Version specifics

A censored version was also released, however the featured version is uncensored.

Others

Featured version: 1.2, 3DFX patch v. 1.1

Two expansion packs were released: Twin Dragon and Wanton Destruction but they used original CD Audio music.
