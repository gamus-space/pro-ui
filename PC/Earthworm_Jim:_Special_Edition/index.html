About

This is a 2D shooting platform game. Jim is an earthworm wearing a special suit. His quest is to meet Princess What's-Her-Name.

The Special Editions extends some levels, adds a new level and provides a new weapon.

Music

Game has CD Audio tracks composed by:

  • Tommy Tallarico Studios

Graphics

The game uses a resolution of 320x240 pixels with 256 colors.

Version specifics

The PC version does not have noticeable differences to other platforms.
