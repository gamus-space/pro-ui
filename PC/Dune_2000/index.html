About

This is a remake of one of the very first RTS games: Dune II.

Three houses fight for control of planet Arrakis:

  • Atreides (blue)
  • Ordos (green)
  • Harkonnen (red)

Music

The music is composed by:

  • Frank Klepacki

The music is stored in AUD proprietary format. It has quality of 22kHz 16bit mono and uses ADPCM compression.

Lots of music is remade from Dune II.

Graphics

Game uses resolution of 640x400 pixels with 64k colors.

Version specifics

The PC version was the main release.
